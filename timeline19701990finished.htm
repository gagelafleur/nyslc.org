Constructing The Timeline 1970 - 1990
 
 
 
       
 

Click any image to enlarge
 

Timeline 1970 - 1990 was created by incarcerated youth at Monroe County Jail working with a NYSLC writer and scholar, a
NYSLC visual artist, a NYSLC recording engineer, and Rochester City School District teachers. An intern from St. John Fisher
College assisted.  Timeline 1970 - 1990 was made possible with funding from The Guido and Ellen Palma Foundation,
The Marie C. and Joseph C. Wilson Foundation, and the Mary S. Mulligan Charitable Trust.
