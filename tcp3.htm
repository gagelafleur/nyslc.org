Goals

Literacy is embedded in every phase of THE COMMUNICATION PROJECT, from writing and illustrating children’s books, to writing and reading for CDs, to writing and performing in plays.  The Goals are:

  • To reinforce basic literacy and the New York State English Language Arts Standards

  • To develop strategies to engage the reluctant learner

  • To develop critical and analytical thinking skills

  • To expand horizons

  • To develop a sense of self, well being, and community belonging
