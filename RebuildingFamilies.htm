Inmates on “Rebuilding Families” NYSLC’s Partnership with the Office of The Sheriff and Rochester Broadway Theater League
 

This program really got me thinking about my kids. I am encouraged to reconnect with my children. This tells me it is not too late, and there is hope. I am so pleased this type of experience and education is available to us in the situation we are in. Congratulations on a job so well done.

            Terrance

 

I am a parent in jail. My kids come to see me. They always wish that I was home.  Now there is something I can give to them when I get out.

            Anonymous

 

It is the poor who suffer the most, the people whose income doesn’t even take care of basic needs. Our kids are always in the middle. This programs gives me something I can do for my kids. 

            Jerry

 

I have pulled my son into Hell through no fault of his own. I want my son to be proud of me. I want to be proud of me. This gives me something I can be proud of with my son. It says “Daddy loves you.” 

            Dwayne

 

NYSLC is an excellent program. I believe “Rebuilding Families” should be a national model. There are so many children in the U.S. with incarcerated parents. This gives us something to give to our children who did nothing and have gone through our struggle. 

            Hendrik

 

This helps me to step up and become a better father for my kids. 

            Tyreek

 

Thank God for a program like this.  It gives me a chance to share how I feel, my true feelings, not feelings of anger because I feel like a failure. I love my children more than life itself. Now I finally have a chance to give something to them to show them my feelings. It feels great to know there are still people who care in the world. Thank God.

            Antonio

 

I am so glad this program exists. Thank you. Thank you for everything. 

            Jermyn

 

I feel this program is the most positive way to reconnect broken families. If there were more of these programs, there would be more understanding of the need to reconnect broken families. People need to understand there are people who fall short  of what they really want to happen in their lives. They  have feelings, and  it’s hard to show them. This program gives us a chance to express ourselves to our children,

            Jacquan

 

This program opened my eyes. I am the child of an incarcerated parent. It is worth everything. I felt the totality of this program on a personal level, and I can only imagine the emotional impact of bonding with my children.

            Dolph 

 

This program is very important. It gives us a reason to make dreams for ourselves while we are incarcerated. It is easy to do nothing in here. Dale, John, Bob truly care to see us change our lives for the good. 

            Eric

 

This program is trying to help families reconnect, and I believe it is successful. Not only am I able to engage in am activity with my son, but I can give my son something that will open new worlds for  him. My son loves music and singing, and I am so happy to know that this program is available to my family. Thank you for what you are giving us.

            Dwayne
