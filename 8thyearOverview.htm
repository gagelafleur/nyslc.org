HOW TO GET FROM HERE TO THE REST OF THE WORLD

 

The title of NYSLC’s partnership with the Office of The Sheriff, County of Monroe at Monroe Correctional Facility, 2013 – 2104, was “How To Get From Here To The Rest of The World.” The project with five artists, writers, a visual artist, and theater artists, focused on reentry back into the Rochester community. The goals included: To engage newly released inmates with their community; To identify elements of what is needed for a successful transition project.  “How to Get From Here To The Rest of The World” was a Pilot Program designed to connect NYSLC’s projects in Monroe Correctional Facility classrooms to the community.

 

“How To Get From Here To The Rest of The World” included introducing, reading, and discussing Poverty and The Concentration of Poverty in the Nine County Greater Rochester Area. Edward Doherty Principal Author and Researcher (Rochester Area Community Foundation and ACT Rochester. Rochester Area Community Foundation, December 2013 http://www.racf.org/CommunityImpact/CommunityImpact/tabid/164/smid/605/ArticleID/101/reftab/36/Default.aspx) in two classrooms, one male and one female.
